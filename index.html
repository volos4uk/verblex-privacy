Privacy Policy · Last updated: July 18, 2026

Overview

Verblex is an offline application for learning English irregular verbs. This policy explains what information the app handles, and — just as importantly — what it does not.

Information We Do Not Collect

Verblex does not collect, transmit, or have access to any personal information. Specifically, the app:

  • Does not use any analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting services
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not require account creation, sign-up, or login
  • Does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties, because none is collected in the first place

Local Data Storage

Your learning progress, review statistics, settings, and any custom flashcards you create are stored locally on your own device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to anyone else. Uninstalling the app, or clearing its data through your device settings, permanently deletes it.

Third-Party Services

Verblex currently does not integrate any third-party SDKs — no analytics, no advertising, no cloud services. If this changes in a future update (for example, if advertising is introduced), this policy will be revised in advance to disclose exactly what is collected and by whom, with the change reflected here under a new "last updated" date.

Children's Privacy

Because Verblex does not collect any personal information from any user, regardless of age, no personal data is ever collected from children. The app does not include chat, social features, or any way to contact or be contacted by other users.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted off your device, there is no server-side storage to secure and no risk of your information being exposed through us. Your data is protected by your device's own operating system and security settings.

Your Control Over Your Data

Because all data lives locally on your device, you are always in full control of it. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling Verblex — no request to us is needed.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this policy will be published on this same page, with the "last updated" date revised accordingly. We recommend checking back periodically.
